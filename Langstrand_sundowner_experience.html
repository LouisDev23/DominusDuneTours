Langstrand Sundowner Experience

If you are looking for a unique and unforgettable way to explore the Namib desert, then you should try Dominus Dune Driving, the ultimate scenic adventure in Namibia!

Dominus Dune Driving offers a scenic route through the dunes of the Namib desert just before sunset. You will drive your 4x4 vehicle, guided by a professional instructor, and experience the thrill and beauty of the desert landscape. You will see the changing colours and shadows of the dunes, the diverse flora and fauna, and the spectacular views of the Atlantic Ocean.

But the best part of the adventure is yet to come. As sunset arrives, you will end up on a large dune overlooking the ocean, where you will witness one of the most breathtaking sunsets in the world. You will see the sun dip into the horizon, painting the sky and the water with amazing hues of orange, pink, and purple. You will also enjoy a glass of champagne, while listening to the soothing sound of the waves and feeling the gentle breeze on your face.

Dominus Dune Driving is more than just a driving tour. It is a romantic and relaxing experience that will make you fall in love with the Namib desert. You will also have the opportunity to take photos, chat with other adventurers, and learn more about the desert from our friendly and knowledgeable instructors.

Dominus Dune Driving is the perfect way to end a day in the Namib desert. Whether you are a solo traveller or a couple, you will find something to suit your taste and budget. We look forward to seeing you soon!

WHAT TO BRING ALONG:

  • A camping chair so that you can sit, relax, and enjoy the beautiful sunset.
